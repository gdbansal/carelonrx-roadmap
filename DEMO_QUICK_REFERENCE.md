## 🎬 Quick Demo Flow (5 Minutes)

### 1. Login (30 sec)
- Show: `https://carelonrx-roadmap1.onrender.com/login.html`
- Highlight: Purple CarelonRx branding
- Action: Sign in

- Show: Statistics cards
- Highlight: Initiatives table with WSJF scores

- Click: "Roadmap" button
- Show: Visual timeline
- Click: Initiative bar
- **Highlight: Hold reason in yellow box** ⚠️
- Show: Export options

- Click: "Analytics" button
- Show: Charts and insights

## 🎤 Script Snippets

### For Hold Reason Feature
> "Notice when we click on an 'On Hold' initiative, the hold reason is prominently displayed in a yellow highlighted box with an alert icon. This ensures stakeholders immediately understand why the initiative is paused - in this case, waiting for UX research and budget approval."

### For WSJF
> "The WSJF score is automatically calculated using the formula: User Business Value plus Time Criticality plus Risk Reduction, divided by Job Size. This helps prioritize initiatives objectively."

### For Permissions
> "Notice the edit and delete buttons are only visible to administrators and the initiative creator - ensuring proper access control."

### For Audit Trail
> "Every change is tracked in the activity log, showing who made what change and when - providing complete transparency and accountability."

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Rehearse** the demo 2-3 times before recording
2. **Speak slowly** - viewers need time to absorb
3. **Use pauses** - let visuals speak
4. **Show, don't tell** - demonstrate features in action
5. **Zoom in** on important details (hold reason box!)
6. **Keep it concise** - 5-7 minutes maximum
7. **End with impact** - show the value proposition
